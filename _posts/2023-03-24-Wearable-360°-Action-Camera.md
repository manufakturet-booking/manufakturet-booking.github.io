The OSMO 360 is a compact camera designed for immersive point-of-view recording. It captures spherical video suitable for documentation and creative projects, providing a full 360° perspective for dynamic environments. Magnetic mounting options allow flexible placement on clothing or gear, and the included dock offers charging and basic controls. Suitable for sports, fieldwork, and interactive media tasks.

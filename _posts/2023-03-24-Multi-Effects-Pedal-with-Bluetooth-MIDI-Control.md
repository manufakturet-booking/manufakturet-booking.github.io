The Eventide H90 is a versatile effects unit intended for advanced audio processing in musical contexts. It supports a wide range of algorithms and includes Bluetooth MIDI connectivity, which makes it suitable for integration into experimental setups or custom control systems. This flexibility allows it to be used in research projects involving interactive audio and real-time signal manipulation.

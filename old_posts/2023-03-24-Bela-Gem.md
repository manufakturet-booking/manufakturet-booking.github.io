The Bela Gem is a smaller-footprint board for low-latency audio and sensor processing. Compared to the original Bela, it offers improved efficiency and updated hardware for integration into compact or wearable projects, while maintaining real-time audio performance. Suitable for prototyping interactive systems and research in sound processing.

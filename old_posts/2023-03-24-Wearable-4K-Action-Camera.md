The OSMO Nano is a compact, lightweight camera designed for point-of-view recording in motion-based activities. It captures high-resolution video suitable for documentation and creative projects, with a wide field of view and stabilization for smooth footage. Magnetic mounting options allow flexible placement on clothing or gear, and the included dock provides charging and basic controls. Suitable for sports, fieldwork, and immersive video tasks.
